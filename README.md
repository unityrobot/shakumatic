# ShakuMatic

ShakuMatic is a restored JavaScript calculator (originally created in 2008 by `scriptmorti`) designed for making Japanese Shakuhachi and Hocchiku flutes.

It’s pretty self-evident: unlike other flute calculators, this is based on the percentage method. It reflects the more traditional shakuhachi approach of determining hole placement by dividing the total length into proportional segments, similar to the technique of stretching an elastic band along the length of the instrument.

### Features
*   **Unit Conversion:** Automatically calculates between Centimeters, Shaku (Feet), and Inches.
*   **Percentage-Based Calibration:** Users can adjust the specific percentage ratios for each of the five holes to suit different crafting traditions or tuning needs.
*   **Visual Schematic:** Provides a vertical diagram with calculated millimeter positions for quick reference during the boring and drilling process.
*   **Mobile Optimized:** Updated layout for easy use in the workshop.

### Usage
1. Enter the total length of your bamboo in any of the top fields (cm, shaku, or inches).
2. Ensure the "Configuration (%)" percentages match your desired tuning system.
3. Click **Calculate** to generate the drill measurements in millimeters.
